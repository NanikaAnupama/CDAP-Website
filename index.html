About the system

Smarter analytics, less manual work.

Replace manual report building with an AI-driven business assistant that adapts to your data structures.

Mid- to high-end F&B businesses collect large volumes of POS data daily — yet most of it is used only for basic reporting. There is no automated KPI discovery, and strategy creation remains manual and fragmented. The result: businesses are data-rich but insight-poor, relying on intuition instead of intelligence.

Contexta is an AI-powered analytics platform that automates data extraction, analysis, forecasting, and sentiment insights to support faster, affordable, and smarter business decisions. Built in collaboration with BladeGen Tech (Pvt) Ltd, the system integrates four core components: automated product categorisation, KPI extraction with dynamic charts, multi-label sentiment analysis, and context-aware predictive modelling.

At a glance
  • Four modules — Categorisation, KPIs, Sentiment, Forecasting
  • RAG-enhanced business intelligence
  • Multi-Agent AI decision support
  • BladeGen Tech industry partnership (NDA)
  • Multi-lingual NLP incl. Sinhala
  • Confidence-based flagging, no forced predictions

Automated Product Categorisation

Web scraping, OCR, and agentic AI auto-categorise POS items across vendors and cuisines — replacing manual mapping and adapting to changing menu formats.

KPI Recommendation & SQL Engine

A hybrid recommender discovers KPIs from data columns (not assumed), runs statistical gatekeeping, generates SQL, and suggests the right visualisation — traceable, never black-box.

Multi-Label Sentiment Analysis

Fine-tuned BERT identifies mixed Positive / Negative / Neutral sentiment in Sinhala and English Google reviews, with confidence scores and Agentic AI for personalised replies.

ATHENA — Context-Aware Forecasting

Sales forecasting that combines POS data with weather, holidays, and seasonality — then explains why trends happen and recommends what to do next, not just numbers.

Research

Domain.

Project domain, research foundations, and technical approach.

Literature Survey

Traditional business intelligence relies on static templates and manual rules. These older methods miss nuanced trends and handle unstructured data poorly.

Automated classification models improve accuracy but often depend on local, static data files instead of live, context-aware information. When evaluating customer feedback, historical sentiment analysis models struggle with mixed emotions and negation, typically restricting outputs to a single label.

For demand planning, hybrid pipelines capture complex patterns better than basic methods, yet many lack operational integration or clear explanations for why a decision was made.

Research Problem

When managing analytics workflows, you face inefficiencies manually rebuilding KPIs, dealing with inconsistent naming conventions, and trying to interpret unstructured feedback.

Existing template-based automations lack the flexibility to handle changes in data structures and fail to provide contextual reasoning. You are missing an intelligent, automated system that uses live online data for product categorisation, processes complex sentiments in reviews, and translates predictions into risk-aware decisions for your business.

Research Gap

Current analytical solutions isolate different stages of your data pipeline. Automated product categorisation rarely uses dynamic web scraping to update its knowledge base.

Sentiment analysis systems lack multi-lingual support, particularly for low-resource languages like Sinhala, and fail to integrate with AI for real-time response generation.

Forecasting systems frequently produce metrics without calibrated uncertainty or human-in-the-loop explainability, leaving you without clear guidance during volatile periods.

Research Objectives

Main objective

Design a Context-Aware BI System that adapts to different business contexts and delivers personalised insights — transitioning from static automation to intelligent insight generation.

Specific objectives
01
Automate menu categorisation

Use machine learning and web scraping to classify products dynamically without manual mapping.

02
Extract KPIs automatically

Detect relevant KPIs from raw data and recommend the best chart types for each context.

03
Multi-lingual sentiment classification

Use a multi-lingual language model to classify mixed sentiments and generate tailored responses.

04
Strategic forecasting module

Build a forecasting module that translates numerical predictions into strategic actions.

Methodology

The system ingests raw Point of Sale (POS) data, customer reviews, and external context like weather or calendars, then routes them through a layered pipeline.

01 · Categorise

NLP and fuzzy matching dynamically recognise and categorise products by scraping vendor websites.

02 · Detect KPIs

Statistical and contextual analysis detects potential KPIs, suggesting the most useful groupings and visualisations.

03 · Analyse Sentiment

A fine-tuned multi-lingual language model identifies sentiment intensity and triggers AI to output tailored replies.

04 · Forecast & Recommend

Time-series forecasting models predict future sales, apply anomaly detection, and translate forecasts into natural language recommendations.
